Mons Olympians CSCI 320: User-Interface Design

Little Green Men is a Unity based game around space travel to the planet Mars. Our target audience is softly focused on students in middle and high school. This will allow us to strengthen core physics concepts in youth while not being annoying to adults who have not studied space exploration.

Space travel has notoriously been too expensive and too dangerous to send humans on a months long, interplanetary mission to mars. However, with a renewed and energetic interest in private companies like SpaceX and Blue Origin a trip to Mars for by the average person is becoming a realistic possibility in our lifetime. This implies a need for enhanced educational tools in educating the next generation of astronauts, scientists, and engineers. The United States has been rejecting science and pushing researchers aside for years which is detrimental to our society as a whole. To ensure the United States is not ignorant on the basics of science, we must begin educating the youth while reinforcing the education through examples of applications.

Our team is comprised of undergraduates at the College of Wooster. We thank Dr. Drew Guarnera for giving us the opportunity to work together to create this game.
